Plan Tailored For You

After you purchase you package, we would like to get to know you a little bit more. We will ask you a series of questions about your interest in Colombia and normal life, afterwards we will compile up a plan that includes locations and advice regarding your move in Colombia. Like the gym? No problem! We are even affiliated with a gym in each city and can help you get a membership!
This plan will be discussed with you at your "Pre-Move Consultation" and a copy will be emailed to you.

Online and In-Person Consultations

As mentioned, we begin an online "Pre Move Consulation" which will include ALL necessary information you need to actually make your move. Advice on flights, housing, safe areas, and day-to-day living are all included. This consultation will also inclide your tailored plan and any other important advice. You will also have the option to meet us in person, where we can go into greater detail when discussing your Tailored Plan and city information. Again, all info in consultations is also sent to you via email. We can even get you a moving service at a discount if you use one of our affiliates!

Guided Tours

Want to the see city you will be living in? Of Course! We offer guided tours that run throughout the city, conducted by experienced tour guides who can answer any and all questions. The tour will also include any locations mentioned in your Travel Plan. We also offer grocery tours where we give advice on deals, and cooking.

Affordable Housing

Need help finding a place to live? We got you covered. We are affiliated with numerous different apartment complexes in Medellín, Bogotá, and Santa Marta. If you use one of our affiliates, you will be guaranteed housing at a great discount.
